Research Projects

  • Attraction and Judgement: Red, Rank, and Romance
    • Mansfield, J., Muqeet, M., Paniagua, D., and Zachocki, E., 2015
    • A study on the effect of the color red on female ratings of attractiveness of a man originally conducted by Elliot and colleagues (2011). Poster presented at Illinois Tech’s Psychology Research Day.
  • The Business of YouTube
    • Professor Limia Shunia and class, 2015
    • Research focused extensively on the history and evolution of YouTube, what makes a successful YouTube channel, and the effective leveraging of YouTube channel for developing new business. Posters presented at Illinois Tech’s Interprofessional Project (IPRO) Day.
  • Recycling, Repurposing, and Reusing: Textile Recycling & the Second-Hand Clothing Trade
    • Potoczak, J. and Zachocki, E., 2014
    • Extensive research on where our clothing goes when we donate them, how to minimize textile waste, and created a solution to promote repurposing old textiles.
